Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about GamePrice AI

Which stores do you support? +

We currently support parsing URLs from Steam, Epic Games Store, GOG, Humble Bundle, Green Man Gaming, Fanatical, and GamersGate. We are constantly working on adding more stores to our database.

Can I cancel anytime? +

Yes, absolutely! You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You will retain access to your plan until the end of your current billing cycle.

Do you support my country? +

We parse store data globally. If a supported store uses regional pricing based on your IP address or account settings, the analysis will typically reflect the currency and price available via the provided link or global defaults.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We securely process payments via Stripe. We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and PayPal for most regions.

How accurate is the price data? +

Our AI scrapes and analyzes real-time data from official store pages and established historical tracking databases to ensure the highest possible accuracy for pricing and deal scores.
